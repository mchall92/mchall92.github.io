About Me

I am currently in my second year of a Master's degree in computer science at Northeastern University.

After completing a degree in medicine and public health, I joined the ALIGN program at Northeastern in 2020. Since then, I have gained fundamental knowledge and technical skills in programming. My ability to learn new skills has always prompted me to take up challenges and I have enjoyed the process of innovative thinking for problem-solving.

I believe the skills I have developed in computer science, my clinical and public health background, and most important of all, my passion to make this a better and healthier world through technology, will enable me to bring different perspectives into the tech world.

My current goal is to gain more real-world experience from co-ops/internships. In the future, I hope to contribute to people's health with the power of technology.
